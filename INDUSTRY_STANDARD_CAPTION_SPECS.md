## üé® **Color Format Conversion**

### Hex to ASS Format
ASS uses `&HAABBGGRR` (Alpha, Blue, Green, Red)

**Examples:**
- `#FFFFFF` (White) ‚Üí `&HFFFFFFFF`
- `#000000` (Black) ‚Üí `&H00000000`
- `#FFD900` (Yellow) ‚Üí `&H00D9FFFF`
- `#00FF00` (Green) ‚Üí `&H00FF00FF`
- `#00F8C8` (Cyan) ‚Üí `&H00C8F8FF`
- `#FF3DA1` (Pink) ‚Üí `&H00A13DFF`
- `#00FF87` (Green) ‚Üí `&H0087FF00`
- `#FFE600` (Yellow) ‚Üí `&H00E6FFFF`
- `#D90000` (Red) ‚Üí `&H000090D9`
- `#FF1493` (Hot Pink) ‚Üí `&H009314FF`

### Transparency
- `&H00` = Fully opaque
- `&H80` = 50% transparent
- `&HFF` = Fully transparent

## üìê **Position & Alignment**

### ASS Alignment Values:
```
7 8 9  (Top)
4 5 6  (Middle)
1 2 3  (Bottom)

1 = Bottom Left
2 = Bottom Center
3 = Bottom Right
4 = Middle Left
5 = Center
6 = Middle Right
7 = Top Left
8 = Top Center
9 = Top Right
```

**Most Common:**
- `alignment=2` ‚Üí Bottom Center (most styles)
- `alignment=5` ‚Üí Center (viral styles, emphasis)
- `alignment=8` ‚Üí Top Center (rare)

## üé¨ **Animation Guidelines**

### Fade In/Out
- **Fast:** 50-80ms (glitch, typing)
- **Normal:** 100-120ms (most styles)
- **Slow:** 150-200ms (elegant, cinematic)

### Karaoke Effect
- Uses `\k` tags with timing in centiseconds
- Example: `{\k50}word {\k50}by {\k50}word`

### Bounce/Zoom
- Scale X/Y: 100% ‚Üí 120% ‚Üí 100%
- Duration: 200-300ms

### Color Change
- Uses `\c` tags for color
- Example: `{\c&H00FF00&}green{\c} normal`
